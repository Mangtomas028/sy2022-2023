What is streaming?

Streaming refers to any media content – live or recorded – delivered to computers and mobile devices via the internet and played back in real-time. It is the continuous transmission of audio or video files from a server to a client. In simpler terms, streaming happens when consumers watch TV or listen to podcasts on Internet-connected devices. With streaming, the media file played on the client device is stored remotely and is transmitted a few seconds at a time over the Internet.

Types of streaming content

  • Music Videos
  • TV and Movies
  • Live Video
  • Podcasts

Platforms used for streaming

There are plenty of platforms used for streaming contents, depending on what content you watch. Here are the main platforms:

  • YouTube - For all types of streaming contents.
  • Facebook - A social networking service used for socializing.
  • Twitch - Made and used mainly for Gaming contents
  • Instagram - A similar platform connected with Facebook, mainly used for photography and videography.
  • TikTok - A popular video-sharing application that allows users to create and watch short videos, many content types are found inside it.
  • Spotify - An application for streaming/listening to music and podcasts.
  • Netflix - Is a subscription-based streaming service that offers a wide variety of movies, documentaries, TV shows & series, cartoons, etc. with different genres from different countries.

    Characteristics of a Good Stream

    Our group believes that willingness is what makes a good stream, it affects the qualities of a good content creator. Qualities like:

    Consistency - Planning the casual and on time streaming (schedule), and having the patience and motivation to follow it. This is the key to keep your audiences involved and engaged.

    Versatility - A versatile content creator would make a good stream, having to adjust with different kinds of contents, being able to entertain your audience’s requests makes your stream relatable and relevant. This also adds up to your experience since you’d be able to discover new things.

    Connection with your audiences - Interaction with your audiences makes your stream enjoyable, which is good since having the bond and connection with your audiences would help create a healthy stream. Another thing is that there should be respect and honesty between a content creator and its audiences..
