Content

The team plans to deliver an educational live streaming content regarding the research that they have conducted about the effects of the excessive use of social media on the daily lives of students.

Services

This study will revolve around 'How excessive use of Social Media impacts/affects the daily lives of the students. Social Media has been a part of life as to its convenient uses and the count just grew during the pandemic. As to the social distance being implemented, many people started to use Social Media to stay in touch and connect with their loved ones. Aside from that, Social Media was and is still being used in gaining information, may it be locally or internationally. As for teens, Social Media can also affect them negatively. Social Media can be a source of entertainment that can lead to not being able to notice time and can be a cause of disrupted sleep if not disciplined. . The team would provide answers to the following questions:

Are the influences of Social Media beneficial or harmful?

What are the factors of excessive use of Social Media?

What are the dangers of Social Media for teenagers?

How does it affect their studies?

How does it affect their time management?

How does it affect their productivity?
